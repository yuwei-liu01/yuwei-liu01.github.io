Yuwei Liu

M.S. Student in Mechanical Engineering (Robotics), Johns Hopkins University

I’m an M.S. student in Mechanical Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, where I work in the ARCADE Lab under the Laboratory for Computational Sensing and Robotics (LCSR).

Prior to this, I earned my bachelor’s degree in Architecture from Wuhan University of Science and Technology and worked as an Assistant Architect at Central South Architectural Design Institute. This background gave me a strong foundation in design thinking, systems-level problem solving, and cross-disciplinary collaboration that continues to inform my approach to engineering.

My research interests lie in autonomous systems, robot learning, and medical robotics, with a particular focus on AI-assisted robotic systems that integrate perception, planning, control, and interaction in real-world environments. I aim to develop robotic systems that are practical, robust, and human-centered. I am currently seeking summer internship opportunities in robotics and AI, while also preparing for Ph.D. applications for Fall 2027.
